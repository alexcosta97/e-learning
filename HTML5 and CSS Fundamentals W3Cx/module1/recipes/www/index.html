Pasta with scrambled eggs and frankfurt sausages

This is a very simple recipe that can be done whilst on a rush.
It is very simple and cheap, but tastes great!

Ingredients

  • Eggs (3 per person)
  • Frankfurt sausages (2 per person)
  • Spaghetti (100gr per person)

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Insert spaghetti and sausages in the boiling water for about 8 min
  3. Whilst the spaghetti and sausages cook, scramble some eggs
